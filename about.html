ABOUT US:

We are a youth-led initiative dedicated to spotlighting queer art history in an accessible way to students and educators.

A. Vogel Castillo

Writer & Editor & Web designer

A. is a senior in Georgia who likes drawing, coding, and listening to queer fiction podcasts. He will never pass up an opportunity to talk about art.

(She is also writing this page. Hello!)


Ellis Schroeder

Writer & Editor

Ellis is a senior in Atlanta, Georgia who is passionate about art, writing, and public health. A queer teen, he explores struggles in his identity through his study of the arts. He aspires to pursue both.



Eva Lucero

Writer & Editor & Designer

Eva is a senior from Georgia who can usually be found drawing, reading, or adding yet another piece of jewelry to her outfit. As a young queer artist interested in pursuing art history and conservation, she wants to share her own passion for art while highlighting the rich history of queerness in the arts that is often overlooked.
